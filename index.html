2. Bharatnatyam


Bharatnatyam is an Indian classical dance. It is a very energetic dance. It is based on jathis which are the beats and on Navras, the Emotions.It is a lovely dance and many of its dances like Natraj Anjali, Pushpanjali, Shabdam etc. are based on Hindu Gods and Godesses.
